# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct / 贡献者公约行为准则

## Our Pledge / 我们的承诺

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

作为成员、贡献者和领导者，我们承诺让每个人都能在无骚扰的环境中参与社区活动，不论年龄、体型、残疾、种族、性别特征、性别认同与表达、经验水平、教育程度、社会经济地位、国籍、外貌、种族、宗教或性取向。

## Our Standards / 我们的标准

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

* Using welcoming and inclusive language / 使用友好和包容的语言
* Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences / 尊重不同的观点和经历
* Gracefully accepting constructive criticism / 优雅地接受建设性批评
* Focusing on what is best for the community / 关注对社区最有利的事情
* Showing empathy towards other community members / 对其他社区成员表现出同理心

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

* The use of sexualized language or imagery / 使用性暗示的语言或图像
* Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments / 恶意挑衅、侮辱或贬损评论
* Public or private harassment / 公开或私下骚扰
* Publishing others' private information without permission / 未经许可发布他人私人信息

## Scope / 适用范围

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, and also applies when
an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces.
Examples of representing our community include using an official e-mail address,
posting via an official social media account, or acting as an appointed
representative at an online or offline event.

本行为准则适用于所有社区空间，也适用于个人在公共空间正式代表社区的情况。
代表社区的示例包括使用官方电子邮件地址、通过官方社交媒体帐户发布信息，
或在线上/线下活动中担任指定代表。

## Enforcement / 执行

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior should be
reported **privately** to the project maintainer via email: lan-desk-security@bbyybb.dev

违反行为准则的情况应通过电子邮件**私下**向项目维护者报告：lan-desk-security@bbyybb.dev

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The maintainer is
obligated to respect the privacy of the reporter of any incident.

所有投诉将被及时、公正地审查和调查。维护者有义务尊重任何事件报告者的隐私。

## Enforcement Guidelines / 执行指南

The project maintainer will follow these guidelines in determining consequences
for any action deemed in violation of this Code of Conduct:

项目维护者将遵循以下指南来确定对任何违反本行为准则行为的处理方式：

### 1. Correction / 纠正

**Community Impact / 社区影响**: Use of inappropriate language or other behavior deemed
unprofessional or unwelcome. / 使用不当语言或其他被认为不专业或不受欢迎的行为。

**Consequence / 后果**: A private, written warning, providing clarity around the nature
of the violation and an explanation of why the behavior was inappropriate. /
私下书面警告，说明违规行为的性质以及行为不当的原因。

### 2. Warning / 警告

**Community Impact / 社区影响**: A violation through a single incident or series of actions. /
通过单次事件或一系列行为造成的违规。

**Consequence / 后果**: A warning with consequences for continued behavior. No interaction
with the people involved for a specified period of time. /
带有后续行为后果的警告。在指定时间内不得与相关人员互动。

### 3. Temporary Ban / 临时封禁

**Community Impact / 社区影响**: A serious violation of community standards. /
严重违反社区标准。

**Consequence / 后果**: A temporary ban from any sort of interaction or public
communication within the community for a specified period of time. /
在指定时间内临时禁止在社区内进行任何形式的互动或公开交流。

### 4. Permanent Ban / 永久封禁

**Community Impact / 社区影响**: Demonstrating a pattern of violation of community
standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior or harassment. /
表现出持续违反社区标准的模式，包括持续的不当行为或骚扰。

**Consequence / 后果**: A permanent ban from any sort of public interaction within
the community. / 永久禁止在社区内进行任何形式的公开互动。
